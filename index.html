Our Story

Stackly started in a rented commercial kitchen with three snack recipes and a delivery bike. Today we formulate, manufacture, and ship across 65 countries — but the goal hasn’t changed: make the everyday stuff genuinely good, and get it to people fast.

Why Stackly

Sourced right. Shipped fast.

We work directly with growers and small manufacturers instead of anonymous supply chains, which means shorter lead times, fresher ingredients, and fewer middlemen marking up the price along the way.

  • Traceable sourcing — every ingredient batch is logged back to its origin farm or supplier.
  • Third-party tested — independent labs verify every production run before it ships.
  • Carbon-aware logistics — regional fulfillment hubs cut delivery distance by 40%.

The Process

01
